Who We Are

Kwolmo Enterprises Ltd is a community-driven social enterprise committed to empowering vulnerable groups across Kenya through training, innovation, and sustainable income-generating programs. We provide opportunities for orphans, widows, youth, and people with disabilities to achieve independence and self-reliance.

Our team focuses on vocational skills, mentorship, and entrepreneurship, enabling long-term community transformation.

Our Mission & Vision

Mission: Empowering communities for a better tomorrow through education, empowerment, and innovation.

Vision: A sustainable and self-reliant society where every individual has equal access to opportunities.

We partner with organizations, local leaders, and donors to create meaningful and measurable change.
